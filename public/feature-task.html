Task Management

Never miss another assignment or deadline

Keep Track of Every Assignment

ZenTask's Task Management system is designed to help students stay on top of all their assignments, projects, and responsibilities with ease. No more forgotten homework or missed deadlines.

Our intelligent system automatically organizes tasks by due date, priority, and subject, making it easy to see what needs attention first. With visual progress tracking and smart reminders, students always know what's coming up and when to start working.

What makes ZenTask's task management special

Smart Prioritization

Automatically sorts tasks based on due dates, importance, and estimated completion time to help students focus on what matters most.

Customizable Reminders

Set personalized reminders based on assignment type and your work habits to ensure you start and complete tasks at the right time.

Progress Tracking

How It Works

A simple process for effective task management

1

Add Tasks

Quickly add homework assignments, projects, tests, and other tasks. Include due dates, subject tags, and priority levels. ZenTask can also automatically import assignments from common school platforms.

2

Organize Automatically

ZenTask intelligently organizes your tasks by due date, subject, and priority. The AI even suggests when to start working on assignments based on your schedule and the estimated time needed.

3

Receive Reminders

Get smart notifications when it's time to start working on tasks, when deadlines are approaching, or when schedule conflicts arise that might impact your ability to complete assignments.

4

Track Progress

Mark tasks as complete and track your progress over time. See patterns in your work habits and receive suggestions for improvement based on your performance.
