Student Dashboard

A personalized command center for student success

Everything Students Need in One Place

ZenTask's Student Dashboard provides a centralized hub where students can track assignments, view their schedule, monitor academic progress, and manage their priorities - all in one intuitive interface.

With customizable widgets and age-appropriate designs, the dashboard grows with students from elementary through high school, helping them develop crucial organizational skills for life.

Key Benefits

  • Reduce forgotten assignments with visual task trackers and priority indicators
  • Monitor academic progress with real-time grade tracking and goal setting
  • Develop time management skills with visual schedule breakdowns

Dashboard Components

Today's Tasks

A prioritized view of homework, projects, and assignments due today or this week, with visual indicators for urgency and completion status.

Progress Tracker

Visual representation of grades, completion rates, and performance metrics across subjects, with trend analysis and goal tracking.

Schedule View

A snapshot of today's and upcoming activities, classes, and events, with quick links to join virtual sessions or see location details.

Achievement Center

Gamified reward system that celebrates completed tasks, academic milestones, and consistent organization habits.

Notifications Hub

Centralized feed of announcements, message alerts, upcoming due dates, and schedule changes requiring attention.

Study Tools

Quick access to study resources, including note-taking templates, flashcard generators, and focused study timers.
