Centralized Calendar

Your family's single source of truth for all student activities

Never Miss an Important Event Again

ZenTask's centralized calendar brings together every aspect of your student's academic and extracurricular life in one intuitive, color-coded interface.

With automatic import capabilities from school systems, sports team schedules, and other digital calendars, you'll have a complete picture of your student's commitments without manual entry.

Key Benefits

  • Eliminate scheduling conflicts with automatic conflict detection and alerts
  • Coordinate transportation and logistics with shared family view
  • Plan ahead with visual time blocking for homework and study sessions

How It Works

Smart Integration

Connect your student's school portal, team apps, and activity schedules to automatically populate the calendar and keep everything in sync.

Color Coding System

Instantly distinguish between different types of activities with customizable color schemes for academics, sports, clubs, family events, and more.

Smart Notifications

Receive customized alerts for upcoming events, changes to schedules, and potential conflicts based on your family's preferences.
