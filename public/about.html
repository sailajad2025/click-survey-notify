About ZenTask

Learn about our mission to simplify student activity management

Our Story

ZenTask was born from a simple observation: families today are juggling more activities than ever before, and the tools to manage them haven't kept pace with the complexity of modern student life.

Founded in 2022 by a group of parents, educators, and tech specialists, our mission is to create a comprehensive platform that brings clarity and calm to the often chaotic world of student activity management.

We're passionate about helping students develop crucial organizational skills while giving parents the visibility they need without micromanaging. By creating intuitive technology that works across devices and integrates seamlessly with school systems, we're building a solution that grows with families throughout their educational journey.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Family-Centered Design

We build solutions that work for real families, not idealized ones. Our platform adapts to your unique family dynamics and needs.

Privacy First

We protect student data with the highest standards of security and privacy, giving families control over their information.

Accessibility for All

We believe organizational tools should be available to every student, regardless of their background or abilities.

Growth Mindset

We help students develop lifelong organizational skills, not just manage tasks for today.
