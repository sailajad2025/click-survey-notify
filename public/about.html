Our Story

ZenTask was born from a simple observation: managing a child's academic life is challenging. Between homework, extracurricular activities, and keeping track of academic progress, parents often feel overwhelmed while students struggle to stay organized.

Founded in 2023, our team of education specialists and tech innovators came together with a shared vision - to create a tool that simplifies student activity management while fostering academic success and reducing stress for families.

Today, ZenTask is helping families across the country transform how they approach education management, creating more harmonious households and empowering students to take ownership of their learning journey.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work at ZenTask

Simplicity

We believe that effective tools should be intuitive and easy to use. ZenTask is designed to simplify complex processes, not add to the chaos.

Educational Empowerment

We're committed to creating solutions that empower students to take ownership of their education while providing parents with the visibility they need.

Family Harmony

By reducing the stress of academic management, we help create more harmonious family dynamics where education becomes a collaborative journey.

Continuous Improvement

We continuously listen to feedback from students, parents, and educators to refine our platform and better serve our community.
