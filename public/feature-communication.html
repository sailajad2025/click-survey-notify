Communication Center

Connect parents, teachers, and students with seamless messaging

Streamlined Communication for Better Outcomes

ZenTask's Communication Center brings together all educational conversations in one secure, organized platform - eliminating the need to track messages across multiple apps and email accounts.

With built-in translation for over 100 languages, document sharing, and thread organization, communication between home and school has never been more effective.

Key Benefits

  • Reduce communication gaps with instant translation for diverse communities
  • Keep all educational conversations organized by student, subject, and topic
  • Share documents, assignments, and resources in a secure environment

Communication Tools

Direct Messaging

Send and receive messages with teachers, coaches, and school administrators in a unified inbox, with message status tracking and read receipts.

Resource Sharing

Share and access important documents, assignments, permission slips, and educational resources with built-in preview and annotation tools.

Language Translation

Break down language barriers with automatic translation of messages in over 100 languages, ensuring all families can engage regardless of their primary language.
